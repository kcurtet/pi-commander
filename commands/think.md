Think deeply about the following problem or question. Do NOT write any code or take any action yet.

**Analysis process:**

1. **Understand the problem**
   - What is being asked?
   - What are the constraints?
   - What assumptions am I making?

2. **Explore the space**
   - What are possible approaches?
   - What are the trade-offs of each?
   - What could go wrong?

3. **Consider alternatives**
   - Are there simpler solutions?
   - What would happen if we did nothing?
   - What has been tried before?

4. **Identify risks**
   - What could break?
   - What edge cases exist?
   - What dependencies are involved?

5. **Recommend approach**
   - Which solution is best and why?
   - What are the next steps?
   - What questions remain unanswered?

Be thorough. Take your time. Ask clarifying questions if needed.
