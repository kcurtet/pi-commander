Given the following problem or current approach, propose alternative solutions:

**For each alternative:**

1. **Description**: What is this approach?
2. **Pros**: What are the advantages?
3. **Cons**: What are the disadvantages?
4. **Complexity**: How difficult is it to implement? (Low/Medium/High)
5. **Risk**: What could go wrong?

**Generate at least 3 alternatives:**
- The obvious/standard approach
- A simpler/minimal approach
- A more ambitious/comprehensive approach
- An unconventional/creative approach

**Compare:**
Create a summary table comparing all alternatives

**Recommendation:**
Which would you choose and why? Consider:
- Project timeline
- Team expertise
- Long-term maintenance
- Scalability needs

Be creative but practical. Not every problem needs a complex solution.
